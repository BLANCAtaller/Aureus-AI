Ketones Δ (Weekly Change)

How does this metric work?

This indicator tracks your metabolic progress by comparing your weekly ketosis hours against the previous week.

Example Calculation:

  • If you did 4h of ketosis this week and 2h last week, you'll see +100%.
  • If you did 3h this week and 4h last week, you'll see -25% as a warning.

It's designed to help you maintain consistency and warn you if your metabolic intensity is dropping.
